Jazz Night Live

Date: May 10, 2025

Time: 7:00 PM – 10:00 PM

Venue: Brisbane Music Hall

Price: $35 per ticket

Tickets Available: 18

Status: Open

Join us at the Brisbane Music Hall in Kangaroo Point for a smooth night of live jazz, featuring talented local musicians and special guest ensembles. Set along the Brisbane River, this iconic venue offers stunning skyline views, a laid-back atmosphere, and licensed dining. Whether you're a long-time jazz lover or a first-time guest, this event promises a rich musical experience in one of the city's most unique settings.
